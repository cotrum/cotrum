my journey in tech...

As a sophomore Computer Science and IT & Web Science major at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in the capital region of New York, I use my technical and communication skills to assist local businesses as a software engineer, obtain CompTIA and ISC2 certifications, and collaborate with teams to develop a range of creative solutions from financial to community-building platforms.

My education and hands-on experience as a member of the ISC2 Capital District NY Chapter, Rensselaer Cybersecurity Collaboratory, and Association for Computing Machinery - Women reflect a commitment to staying at the forefront of technology, with aspirations to contribute to the evolving intersection of cybersecurity and AI/ML applications in the industry.

art.

In need of a unique gift? In love with your pet? Try a highly personalized and stylized realistic painted portrait!

commission fast facts

  • Oil paint (water-soluable)
  • 8"x10" canvas, 0.75" depth
  • $70
  • Custom sizes available--please contact

I specialize in creating vibrant and captivating paintings of any subject. I love experimenting with color using my water-soluable oil paints (environmentally-safe, faster drying, and just as buttery as traditional oils!) especially via human and animal portraits. Each piece is crafted with meticulous attention to detail based on your provided reference image.

The client may request a custom canvas size. Price will depend on said size and complexity of the piece. Large works require a 25% upfront fee.

I hope you consider commissioning me!
